Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Eventify

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation. You'll need to provide your full name, email address, choose a role (Student or Admin), and create a password. Once verified, you can start using Eventify.

What's the difference between Student and Admin roles?

Students can browse events, register for events, manage their registrations, and view their dashboard. Admins can create and manage events, view attendee lists, and access analytics and management tools.

How do I log in to my account?

Use the "Login" button in the top navigation. Enter your email address and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

Event Registration

How do I register for an event?

Browse events on the Events page or from the home page. Click on an event to view details, then click the "Register" button. You'll receive a confirmation and the event will appear in your dashboard.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up until the event's registration deadline. Go to your dashboard or the event page and click "Unregister" or "Cancel Registration".

What happens if an event is full?

If an event reaches its capacity, the registration button will be disabled and show "Full". You can check back later as spots may become available if other students cancel their registrations.

Is there a deadline for registration?

Yes, each event has a registration deadline set by the organizer. You cannot register after this deadline has passed. The deadline is clearly displayed on each event page.

Event Management (Admin)

How do I create a new event?

As an Admin, go to your Admin Dashboard and click "Create Event". Fill out the event details including title, description, date/time, location, category, and capacity. You can also upload an event poster image.

Can I edit an event after creating it?

Yes, you can edit event details from the Admin Dashboard. Click on the event and select "Edit". You can modify most details, but be careful about changing dates or times if students have already registered.

How do I manage event attendees?

From the Admin Dashboard, click on an event and select "Attendees". You can view the list of registered students, mark attendance, export attendee data, and remove registrations if needed.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported?

Eventify works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your browser.

Is Eventify mobile-friendly?

Yes! Eventify is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. You can browse events, register, and manage your account from any smartphone or tablet.

What should I do if I encounter an error?

Try refreshing the page first. If the problem persists, check your internet connection and try again. For persistent issues, you can contact support through the Help Center.

Privacy & Security

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, Eventify uses industry-standard security measures. Your password is encrypted, and we use Firebase's secure authentication system. We never share your personal information with third parties.

Who can see my event registrations?

Only you and the event organizers (Admins) can see your registrations. Other students cannot see which events you've registered for unless you choose to share that information.
